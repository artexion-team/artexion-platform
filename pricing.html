FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our pricing and plans

Can I change plans later?

Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. Changes take effect immediately, and we'll prorate any differences in billing.

What happens if I exceed my usage limits?

We'll notify you before you reach your limits. For Pro plans, overages are billed at $0.002 per additional execution. Enterprise plans have custom overage arrangements.

Is there a free trial for paid plans?

Yes, we offer a 14-day free trial for our Pro plan. No credit card required to start. You'll get full access to all Pro features during your trial.

Do you offer discounts for startups or nonprofits?

Yes, we offer special pricing for eligible startups, educational institutions, and nonprofit organizations. Contact our sales team for more information.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards, PayPal, and bank transfers for annual plans. Enterprise customers can also pay via invoice.

Can I get a refund if I'm not satisfied?

We offer a 30-day money-back guarantee for annual Pro plans. If you're not satisfied, contact our support team for a full refund.
